Nutrition & Food Aid

Feeding Programs for Children & Vulnerable Families

At Cheherma Foundation, we believe that access to nutritious food is a basic human right. Our Nutrition and Food Aid Program is designed to combat hunger, improve health outcomes, and support vulnerable populations in underserved communities. We provide nutrient-rich meals, food baskets, and educational resources to individuals and families facing food insecurity.

What We Do

Food Distribution

  • Monthly food packages to low-income families
  • Hot meal services for children, seniors, and those experiencing homelessness
  • Emergency food relief during crises and disasters

Nutrition Education

  • Healthy cooking workshops
  • Nutritional counselling and diet planning
  • Educational campaigns on food hygiene and dietary diversity

Sustainable Support

  • Support for community gardens and urban farming
  • Partnerships with local farmers and food banks
  • Advocacy for policies promoting food justice

Who We Serve

  • Low-income families and single-parent households
  • Elderly individuals on fixed incomes
  • Newcomers, slum dwellers, and marginalized communities
  • School-aged children and youth at risk of malnutrition

Program Impact

Since launching the program:

  • 4,200+ food baskets distributed
  • 120+ nutrition workshops conducted
  • 2,500 individuals reached with direct food aid

Get Involved

Support us by:

  • Donating funds or non-perishable food items
  • Volunteering at food drives and distribution centers
  • Becoming a community nutrition ambassador

Our Vision

To build a hunger-free, healthier community where everyone has the knowledge and resources to live a nourished life.
