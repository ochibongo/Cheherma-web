Health Outreach

This program delivers free healthcare and medical education to slum communities like Kibera, Nairobi. Our goal is to identify prevalent health issues and provide sustainable solutions through partnerships with local and global medical professionals.

Program Objectives

  • Provide free, high-quality medical checkups and treatment
  • Raise awareness on chronic and infectious diseases
  • Promote hygiene to prevent disabling diseases like dental and eye conditions
  • Refer severe cases for advanced care
  • Support local health campaigns and share data with authorities

No effort is too small. No border too far. Be the Healing Hands the World Needs

Join Our Global Health Outreach Mission

At Cheherma Foundation, we believe access to healthcare is a basic human right — not a privilege. Through free medical screenings, mobile wellness clinics, and grassroots health awareness campaigns, we are reaching the underserved, one village at a time.

We are calling on compassionate medical professionals like you — doctors, nurses, dentists, lab technologists, pharmacists, and public health workers — to share your expertise and bring quality care where it’s needed most.
