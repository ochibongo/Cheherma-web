Who We Are

The Cheherma Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities through health, education, and social development initiatives.

Free Medical & Dental Programs

We offer mobile clinics, screening camps, and basic treatment to communities with limited access to healthcare.

Education Promotion & Sponsorship

We provide learning materials, mentorship, and scholarship opportunities for underprivileged children.

Telemedicine & Digital Health Access

Expanding digital healthcare services to bridge the gap in rural and marginalized communities.

Community Pharmacy Services

Providing access to essential medicines and pharmacist-led education in underserved areas.

Advocacy & Rights Awareness

We empower individuals to understand their rights and participate in health and civic matters.

Non-Communicable Disease Prevention & Control

Health education, screenings, and lifestyle interventions to reduce the burden of chronic illnesses.

For general inquiries, email us at info@cheherma.org. For other matters, please fill out our contact form, and we will get back to you promptly.
