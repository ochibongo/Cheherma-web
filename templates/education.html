Education Promotion & sponsorship

CheHerma Student Sponsorship Fund enables the organization to provide access to quality education to selected vulnerable children from extremely poor families within the slum settlements. Under this program, the organization formulates initiatives such as monthly fundraisers and one off donations from well wishers that will enable us to offer educational support from primary school levels to collage levels . Sponsored children are selected through home visits which we at CheHerma do as part of having an understanding of the needs and their wellbeing. Vulnerable children enrolled under this program will also benefit from our healthcare programs.

The main utilization of the funds is to support our school children with

  • School uniforms
  • Exercise books and stationery
  • Healthcare and check-ups
  • Sanitary towels for our girls
  • Psycho-social support (mentorship)
  • Empower girls to stay in school and pursue STEM careers
  • Organize community literacy and after-school tutoring programs

No child should defer their dreams because of lack of school fees, books and uniform!
